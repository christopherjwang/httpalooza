Jam out with HTTPalooza

With a simple, natural DSL, HTTPalooza is easy to get started with:

require 'httpalooza'

response = HTTPalooza.invite.patron_to_the_stage.get('http://httpalooza.com')

puts response[:patron][:body]

But when you have so many HTTP clients to choose from, why limit yourself?

response = HTTPalooza.invite.patron_to_the_stage.
  with_special_guests_curb_and_net_http.get('http://httpalooza.com')

puts response[:patron][:body]
puts response[:curb][:body]
puts response[:net_http][:body]
